Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and lens technology.

Shipping & Delivery

For non-prescription sunglasses and frames, orders typically ship within 24 hours and arrive in 3-5 business days. For prescription lenses, please allow 7-10 business days for our lab to craft and install your custom lenses before shipping.

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping usually takes 10-15 business days depending on customs in your country. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 14-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your glasses, you can return them for a full refund or exchange within 14 days of delivery. The frames must be in their original, unworn condition.

Because prescription lenses are custom-made for your eyes, the lens cost is non-refundable. However, we will gladly refund the cost of the frame if returned within our 14-day window.

Prescriptions & Lenses

You can upload a photo or PDF of your prescription during the checkout process. Alternatively, you can email it to us at rx@visioncraft.com with your order number. Please ensure your prescription includes your Pupillary Distance (PD).

Yes, all our prescription lenses come standard with a premium scratch-resistant coating and 100% UV protection. Anti-glare (anti-reflective) and blue light blocking coatings can be added during the lens selection step.
